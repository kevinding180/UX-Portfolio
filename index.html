About Me

As a UX designer with a background in game design and development, I bring a unique perspective to user experience design. My understanding of game mechanics and player engagement allows me to create designs that not only solve real problems but also engage and delight users. My skills in user research, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing are paired with a deep understanding of Unity, Unreal Engine, and other game engines. I am also proficient in design tools such as Sketch, Adobe XD, and Figma. I strive to create designs that are not only functional but also immersive and enjoyable for the end-users. My ultimate goal is to create experiences that are not only useful but also enjoyable, memorable and emotionally engaging.
